RESPECTING YOUR PRIVACY

At Devnexis, we hold your privacy in the highest regard. We are dedicated to adhering to applicable laws and regulations that govern the collection of personal information. This privacy policy, explains how we handle and protect the information we gather from you on our website.

COLLECTING INFORMATION

We collect personal information that you willingly provide when contacting us or applying for job positions through our website. Additionally, while using our website, your devices may automatically send us information like your IP address, browser type, visited pages, and other technical details.

LOG DATA

When you visit our website, your web browser provides us with standard log data. While this information may not directly identify you, it may be combined with other data to do so. This data helps us analyze and enhance the performance and user experience of our website.

INFORMATION USAGE

We collect and use personal information for purposes such as ensuring security, preventing fraud, and guaranteeing the appropriate use of our website according to our terms of use. We may also combine the information we collect with general data from trusted sources for analysis and research purposes.

RETENTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this privacy policy. Once your information is no longer required, we either delete it or anonymize it by removing identifiable details. However, we may retain your information if obligated to do so by law, accounting standards, or for research and statistical purposes.

GET IN TOUCH

Should you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please don't hesitate to contact us at info@devnexis.com . We are here to address any inquiries you may have.
